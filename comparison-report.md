## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

The current veracitytech.com has a solid content foundation — good copy, real client testimonials, award badges (CRN MSP 500, Channel Partners MSP 501), and 6 FAQ entries using legacy Microdata schema. However, it lacks the structured data infrastructure, AI optimization, and content depth that modern search engines and AI platforms require to surface content in search results, AI-generated answers, and voice search responses.

The new site retains everything that works about the current site and layers on 183+ JSON-LD schemas, 132 FAQ question-and-answer pairs, 45 city-specific landing pages, 4 deep industry pages, 10 SEO-optimized blog articles, a full AI crawler optimization suite, and interactive lead generation tools — all designed to make Veracity Technologies the most findable managed IT provider in the Twin Cities metro.
